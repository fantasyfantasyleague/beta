We need to rework combat system.
New way: when you attack there are two rolls that happen.
First you roll to hit, this is ATK vs DEF as it is right now.
Second, you roll to determine dmg. This is based on ATK, but it can't be too op where everyone is one-shotting. 
This way, if you just barely hit an enemy, you can still do decent dmg.
DEF has no effect on dmg, only on if a unit is hit or not.
